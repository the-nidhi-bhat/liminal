‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our community meetups

Is this really free? +
Yes! All community meetups are completely free. Our mission is to make mental health support accessible to everyone. Some special workshops may have minimal costs to cover venue or materials, but regular meetups are always free.
Are the meetings confidential? +
Absolutely. What's shared in the circle stays in the circle. All our facilitators are trained in confidentiality practices, and we have strict community guidelines to ensure everyone feels safe.
Do I need to speak or share my story? +
No pressure at all! You can simply listen and observe until you feel comfortable. Many people start by just being present, and that's completely okay. Share only when and if you want to.
Who facilitates these meetups? +
Our meetups are led by trained peer facilitators with lived experience in mental health challenges, along with licensed counselors and therapists who volunteer their time. All facilitators undergo background checks and training.
How often do meetups happen? +
In each city, we'll have multiple meetups weekly - support circles twice a week, activity-based meetups on weekends, and special events monthly. You can attend as many or as few as you like.
What if my city isn't listed? +
We're expanding rapidly! Select "Other City" in the waitlist form and tell us where you are. If we get enough interest from your area, we'll prioritize launching there. You can also consider starting your own local chapter with our support.
Can I bring a friend? +
Yes! Many people find it easier to attend their first meetup with a friend. Just make sure both of you register so we can plan for the group size.
Is this a replacement for therapy? +
No. While our meetups provide valuable peer support and community, they are not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. We encourage you to work with a therapist or counselor alongside participating in our community.
